About Me

My name is Camelia Shanta anak Giri, and I am a 20-year-old student currently pursuing a Diploma in Business Studies with a specialization in Transport. I am enrolled at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Kota Kinabalu, one of Malaysia’s leading institutions for higher education. My academic journey has been shaped by a keen interest in the dynamic and ever-evolving field of transport and logistics, which plays a pivotal role in connecting industries and driving global commerce. Through my diploma program, I am developing a comprehensive understanding of business operations, management principles, and transport systems, all of which are crucial in today’s interconnected world. As a student, I am highly motivated and dedicated to excelling in my studies. I actively seek opportunities to expand my knowledge and skills, both inside and outside the classroom, to prepare myself for the challenges of the professional world. My goal is to contribute meaningfully to the transport and logistics sector by leveraging innovative solutions and sustainable practices.
